[MU/TH/UR](https://avp.fandom.com/wiki/MU/TH/UR_6000) was a model 282 artificial intelligence operating system used aboard vessels in the Alien universe.

While MU/TH/UR was a 2.1 terabyte mainframe that monitored the Nostromo's flight and environmental functions and this version is a little different.

Our version is starting its life as an Unraid server:

The machine has been purpose built for the task to replace an existing machine `FRANKENMONKEY`. While he has served us well, his time is almost over. A slow i3 with spinning drives and now pooled storage etc just won't cut it in today's world.
